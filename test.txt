### 3. Cost Analysis

| Service | Estimated Cost ($/month) |
|------------|-----------------------------|

| Amazon MSK | $500 - $3,000 |
| Confluent Kafka | $500 - $5,000 |

### Final Takeaways

- **For Enterprise Security:** IBM MQ or Confluent Kafka.
- **For Low Operational Overhead:** Amazon SQS, Firehose, Amazon MQ, Confluent Kafka.
- **For Cost Efficiency:** Amazon SQS, Firehose, MSK.
- **For High-Performance Streaming:** Kafka, Amazon MSK, Confluent Kafka, Amazon Kinesis.
